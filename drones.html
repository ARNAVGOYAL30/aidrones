ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE DRONES

AI Drones largely based on computer vision. It is mainly used in military(Army, Navy, Airforce). This technology enables drones to detect objects while flying and allows the analysis and recording of information on the ground. Computer vision works through high-performance, onboard image processing performed with a neural network.

The drone's operator, remotely monitoring a video feed from the craft, can wait for enemy soldiers or a tank to appear below. In some cases, the drones are equipped with A.I. software that lets them hunt for particular kinds of targets based on images that have been fed into their onboard systems.

Spy Drones are used to surveillance the area, and send all the movement of the objects to the operator. It is mainly used by military(Army, Navy, Airforce) to detect location of the enemy, and their equipment's.

Attacking Drones are used to neutralised the objects, sometimes it is used to surveillance the cross-border area also, in war conditions. It is like multirole drone which is used for spying and attacking.
