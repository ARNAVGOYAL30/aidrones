HOW TO MAKE SPY DRONES?

As you know that spy drones are of two types:
  • Easily Visible & detected
  • Visible & can’t detected

EASILY VISIBLE & DETECTED

To made this type of drones we need some specific material like:
  • Frame
  • Power distribution board
  • Battery
  • Battery monitor
  • Mounting pad
  • Controller
  • Propellers
  • Connectors
  • Camera
  • RC transmitter and receiver
  • USB key
  • Motors: 4 motors are required
  • An electric speed control for delivering power to the motor
  • AWG silicon wire
  • thread locking compound
  • battery charger
  • servo lead wire

CONSTRUCTION

  • Construct a frame is the first thing to do in building your drone, which could be made with plastic, metal, or wood. If you are using wood, cut the wood board of about 70cm and 30-35mm thick and make an X- frame with the board.

  • Drill some holes in the wooden frame to fit in the four motors using the distance which will allow free movement of the clip and shaft of the motor.

  • After mounting the motors, you have to fix the speed controllers at the bottom side of the drone using zip ties then adding the land gear to reduce the risk of shock when landing the drone.

  • The remote controller is needed to control the drone during flight, and various controllers are available nowadays, such as Flysky, Futaba, and so on.

  • After choosing the right controller, you need to fix it on top of the frame in a straight direction for easy control.

  • The camera should be mounted in a forward viewing position. An HD camera is a good fit for a clearer vision. The location of the camera is what makes the spying easy; the front of the drone is the best place to place the camera.

  • Connect the battery to the lower part of the frame; the battery monitor should be fixed to and make sure it is tight so as not to fall off during flight.

    • After fixing all the necessary materials, we have to test our drone to make sure that everything is working properly or not.
